## 5. Qari AI — Tarteel's Most Sophisticated Challenger

- **URL**: qariai.app
- **Differentiator**: Tajweed correction at **phoneme level** — Makharij (articulation points), Madd (elongation), Ikhfa, Idgham, Qalqalah — not just word-level like Tarteel
- **Positioning**: "The only app that combines AI-powered Hifz tracking with real-time Tajweed correction at the phonetic level"
- **Tarteel's gap they exploit**: "Tarteel finds mistakes at word level, not letters, diacritics, or tajweed rules"
- **Reviews roundup source**: [qariai.app/best-ai-quran-app](https://qariai.app/best-ai-quran-app) — their own SEO play comparing themselves favorably

**Implication for Sakina**: Even Qari AI is still async/solo. Neither Tarteel nor Qari AI offers *live session with a teacher*. The human-in-the-loop architecture is still a blue ocean.
